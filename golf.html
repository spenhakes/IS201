Why Golf?

  1. Good Excercise

    2. While golf might not be as intense as some other sports, it still offers a good combination of physical and mental exercise, making it a beneficial activity for maintaining health and fitness.

  2. Challenge Yourself

    3. Golf is a personal sport. Each day you work to improve yourself. There is plenty to learn and so it's something that can be worked on your whole life.

  3. Better Health

    4. Playing golf can increase your exposure to the sun and provide you Vitamin D that helps to:

    • Lower your risk of cancer
    • Boost your immune stystem
    • Help body absorb calcium to prevent bone loss

  4. Builds Character

    5. Golf can be a frustrating game and make you face difficult situations. You character can be made stronger as you work to be better under pressure and when frustrated.
